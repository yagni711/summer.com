Summer.com is an online platform that allows people from all over the world to get a first-hand experience of the glorious Indian ethnic fashion.
The young website currently works extensively with women's fashion - with splendid saree - the cultural Indian ensemble - at its centre. Saree.com believes that these traditional, amazing arts must get the platform they deserve. We also hold Saree, the ubiquitous symbol of Indian fashion, in great esteem, and look for innovative ways to promote it globally.
